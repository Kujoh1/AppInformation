About the app · Last updated: February 2026

What is BabyZoo?

BabyZoo is a mobile app that offers interactive animal animations for young children. It is designed for parents or guardians to use together with their little ones. The app is intended to be operated by adults; content is suitable for very young children.

Features

  • Interactive animals – Simple, child-friendly animations and sounds
  • No account required – Start using the app without signing up
  • Safe and private – No ads, no tracking, no personal data collected
  • Optional subscription – Free content to try; more content available via monthly or annual subscription

For Families

BabyZoo focuses on a calm, age-appropriate experience. The images and sounds in the app were created with licensed AI tools. There are no social features, no in-app messaging, and no location or camera use. All subscription and payment handling is done through the Apple App Store.

Availability

BabyZoo is available on the App Store for iPhone and iPad. Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID; you can cancel or change your subscription at any time in Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.
