2. About the App

BabyZoo is an application designed for parents or guardians to use together with young children. The app is intended to be operated by adults.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 No Account Required

The app does not require registration. No user accounts are created.

3.2 No Personal Data from Children

We do not collect personal data from children. The app does not include:

  • User profiles
  • Messaging features
  • Location tracking
  • Camera or microphone recording
  • Social media integrations

3.3 In-App Purchases

BabyZoo offers optional subscription purchases. All payments are processed exclusively through the Apple App Store. We do not receive or store payment details such as credit card information.

For more information, please review Apple’s Privacy Policy.

3.4 Device Information

When downloading or using the app, Apple may automatically process certain technical information (such as device type or operating system version). This data is handled by Apple. We do not have direct access to this information.

4. AI-Generated Content

The images and sounds used in BabyZoo were created using licensed AI tools. No personal user data is processed or stored in connection with this content.

5. No Advertising or Tracking

The app does not include:

  • Third-party advertising
  • Analytics tracking tools
  • Behavioral tracking
  • Data sharing with third parties

6. Local Data Storage

The app may store limited technical information locally on the device (for example, subscription status). No personal data is stored on external servers.
