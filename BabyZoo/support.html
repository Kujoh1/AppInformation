Frequently Asked Questions

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. On your iPhone or iPad, go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions, select BabyZoo, and choose to cancel or change your plan. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple. You can report a problem or request a refund in the App Store: open the App Store app, tap your profile icon, then Purchase History, find BabyZoo, and use Report a Problem. Apple’s refund policy and consumer rights apply.

Do I need an account to use the app?

No. BabyZoo does not require registration or a user account. You can use the app without signing up. Subscription purchases are linked to your Apple ID only.

The app is not working correctly. What can I do?

Try closing the app completely and reopening it. Ensure your device is updated to a supported iOS version. If the issue continues, contact us at hi@kudesi.de and describe what happens (e.g. screen, action, error message if any).

Where can I find the Privacy Policy and Terms of Use?

You can read our Privacy Policy and Terms of Use on this site. They are also linked from the app.
